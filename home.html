An e-library, or digital library, is a collection of digital resources that are available to users online. E-libraries can include a variety of content, such as: Books, Journals, Articles, Research papers, Multimedia content, E-books, Audiobooks, Videos, Images, and Academic journals They provide students with the flexibility to access information anytime and from anywhere, requiring only a few clicks or taps. E-libraries house a vast array of resources that aid students in expanding their knowledge, honing their research skills, and enhancing their academic performance. This website is just a model of a digital library and provides 12 of the best books in each of the categories allowing users to navigate themselves to the website of the individual book.
